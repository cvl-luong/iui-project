Caprese Chicken Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 slices mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Sear in olive oil over medium heat, 5-6 minutes per side.
  2. Add garlic and cherry tomatoes to the skillet. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Top each chicken breast with mozzarella. Cover and let melt.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
